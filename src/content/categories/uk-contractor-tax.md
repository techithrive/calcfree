  If you contract, subcontract or run a side business in the UK, your tax is rarely as simple as a payslip. CIS deductions come off before your expenses are counted. Umbrella and limited-company routes leave very different amounts in your pocket. A side hustle can quietly tip you into a higher tax band. The tools in this section are built around those real situations rather than generic sums.

  Each calculator shows its working, states what it assumes, and links the relevant HMRC guidance so you can check the rules yourself. They give you a clear estimate to plan around — what a CIS refund might come to, whether an umbrella or your own company keeps more of your day rate, how much to set aside on second-income earnings, or what a company car will cost you in tax. None of them is a substitute for advice from an accountant, and every result says so. The aim is simple: help you understand the number before you make a decision about it.
