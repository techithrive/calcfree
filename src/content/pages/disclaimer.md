Everything on CalcFree is intended to help you understand and plan — nothing more.

## The estimates are guides

Our calculators produce estimates from the figures you enter and a set of stated assumptions. They are not a substitute for professional financial, tax or legal advice, and they cannot see the full picture of your circumstances. Two people with similar inputs can still have very different correct answers once the details are known.

## Figures change

Tax bands, thresholds, allowances and prices change — sometimes more than once a year. We review our data regularly and show a "last reviewed" date on each tool, but there may be periods where a rate has changed and our figure hasn't caught up yet. Always confirm anything important against the official source we link to, such as HMRC, the CRA or the ATO.

## Before you act

If a decision involves a meaningful amount of money or legal obligation — a tax return, a property purchase, a redundancy settlement, an inheritance — treat our number as a starting point and speak to a qualified professional before acting on it.
